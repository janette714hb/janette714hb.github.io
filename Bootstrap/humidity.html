Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

Humidity in January in the equatorial zone doesn't seem to be any different than other latitudinal ranges. According to Quora, "There are no seasons in equatorial climate. Temperature is high due to sun which is directly above the area. High temperatures evaporates the water so that's why its humid and it often results in convectional rain."
